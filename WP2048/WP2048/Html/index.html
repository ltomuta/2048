About/Help

How to play: Swipe left, right, up or down on the board to make the tiles move in the respective direction. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.

Windows Phone 8 port by Lucian Tomuta.
